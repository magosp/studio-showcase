FAQ

Is there a per-event fee?
No. Tiers are priced on provisioned throughput and retention. Volume within your tier ceiling is not metered per message.
Can I change tiers?
Yes — up or down at any time. Throughput and retention apply from the next billing period; replication changes roll out without downtime.
Where does data live?
In the region you provision. Scale tiers can replicate across regions; Dev and Team are single-region.
Do you meter schema registry calls?
No. The registry is part of every tier at no extra charge.
